The Story So Far....

"Established in 2020 by local man Ken Delaney, the Mullingar Hiking Group had its first official outing on Sunday, September 13th of the same year. On that day, a handful of people tackled the picturesque Benbulben in Co. Sligo. Since then, the group has rapidly grown from a small number to over 100 members in less than a year. This inclusive group caters to individuals of all fitness levels, whether you aspire to wander the captivating boardwalks of Glendalough, Co. Wicklow or conquer the summit of Mont Blanc (France) at 4,809m. With annual trips to Snowdon (Wales) and Ben Nevis (Scotland), there are ample opportunities for everyone to embrace exciting challenges. Our Mission

At Mullingar Hiking Group, our mission is to explore the breathtaking landscapes of Ireland throughout the year, while also embarking on exciting international trips. With a shared passion for hiking and adventure, we strive to create memorable experiences for all who join us on our journeys. We firmly believe that getting out into nature is not only a means of exploration but also an avenue for personal growth and enjoyment. Our ethos centers around fostering a welcoming and inclusive community, where individuals of all backgrounds can come together, form lasting friendships, and challenge themselves in the great outdoors. As we traverse Ireland's picturesque trails and conquer majestic mountains, we aim to instill a sense of awe and appreciation for the natural wonders that surround us. Each hike presents an opportunity to connect with the land, to immerse ourselves in its beauty, and to recharge our spirits. In addition to our local adventures, we are committed to organizing international trips that allow us to explore diverse landscapes and cultures worldwide. By broadening our horizons beyond Ireland's borders, we aim to expand our understanding of the world and foster a global perspective. But above all, our ultimate goal is to help people get out and have fun! We believe that hiking is not just a physical activity but a source of joy and rejuvenation. Whether you are a seasoned hiker or a beginner eager to take the first step, we welcome you to join us on this remarkable journey of exploration, camaraderie, and pure adventure. Come along with MHG and let's create memories that will last a lifetime as we discover the wonders of Ireland and beyond, one trail at a time.
